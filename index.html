Tension Detection

Purpose: Tension feedback and adjustment of carbon fibre for COPV manufacturing.

Designed, built, and tested a tension detection prototype. The system uses a load cell and roller guiding to measure the tension of carbon fiber towpreg. The user is then able to adjust the tension as needed using a custom spring-loaded disc brake that I designed.

BMS

Purpose: Develop battery management PCB for Mars Rover battery to enable cell balancing capabilities.

Designed a 4-layer STM32-based PCB in Altium to manage the electrical consumption and recharging of a 12-cell battery. The PCB features two 7 segment displays for battery percent, low battery buzzer, and built-in temperature monitoring.

Fender FEA

Purpose: Redesign fender for industrial harvesting head to support strength concerns.

Performed FEA on steel fender to investigate a concern related to possible deformation. I conducted root cause analysis to identify the cause of the perceived deformation, generated 10+ design iterations, and implemented a final design which reduces deflection by 53% while maintaining mass properties.
